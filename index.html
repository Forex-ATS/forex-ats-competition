About

The Forex ATS Competition Platform invites developers to enhance an automated trading system. Each competition uses profits from our copy trading service to fund the prize pool.

How It Works

  • Submit your improved version of the trading algorithm.
  • Algorithms are evaluated under strict risk and performance criteria.
  • The best-performing submission wins the cash prize.
  • Winning algorithms are deployed to our copy trading service.
  • As profits grow, the prize pool increases and competition conditions evolve.
